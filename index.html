The Process

How It Works

Our institutional acquisition process is designed for efficiency and certainty. From initial review to funded closing — our team handles every detail.

01

Submit Your Property

Complete our brief property intake form. Our acquisitions team will review your submission promptly — no obligation required.

02

Receive Our Offer

Our team evaluates your property and presents a formal purchase offer — typically within 24 to 48 hours of submission.

03

Review & Sign

Upon acceptance, our legal team prepares all documentation. No repairs, no cleanup, no out-of-pocket costs on your end.

04

Close & Get Paid

We coordinate with a trusted closing attorney to finalize the transaction. Funds are delivered at closing — straightforward and guaranteed.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

FutureRoma Holdings LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or submit a property for acquisition consideration. Please read this policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Property Information: Details about land you submit for consideration, including location, size, and condition.
  • Communications: Records of correspondence, including SMS messages, emails, and form submissions.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage data collected automatically when you visit our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Evaluate and make offers on land properties submitted to us.
  • Communicate with you via phone, email, or SMS text message regarding your submission.
  • Process and complete real estate transactions.
  • Improve our website and acquisition services.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. SMS / Text Message Communications

By submitting your phone number through our contact form, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages from FutureRoma Holdings LLC related to your property submission. This may include acquisition updates, offer details, or transaction status notifications.

  • Opt-Out: You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message. You will receive one final confirmation and no further messages will be sent.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance, or contact us at [email protected].
  • Message & Data Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
  • Frequency: Message frequency varies based on the status of your property submission.
  • We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With closing attorneys, title companies, or professionals necessary to complete a transaction you have agreed to.
  • With service providers who assist in operating our website or business, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at [email protected].

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their individual privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
